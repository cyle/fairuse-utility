Emerson College Fair Use Info

Getting Started

This is Emerson College's page about Fair Use and how it applies to you. This is meant to educate the Emerson community about using copyrighted works in and out of the classroom.

This information is especially important now, when we can so easily distribute copies of textbooks digitally and stream full-length movies over the web. This site is meant to help you determine what is within the law, and what is potentially dangerous.

We built a tool to help you determine whether your use of media complies with the four factors of Fair Use. It may prove helpful to have a copy of its output as a record of your intention to comply with copyright law.

Please be aware that this is not legal advice, it is simply an educational guide to help you understand copyright and fair use.
